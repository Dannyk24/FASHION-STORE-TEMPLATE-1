Our Story

Made on the mainland,
worn everywhere.

ASHE started in a one-room studio off Allen Avenue with a single sewing machine and a refusal to make anything we wouldn't wear ourselves. Every piece still passes through the same hands that cut the first collection — checked for drape, weight, and the kind of finishing that survives a Lagos Friday and a London winter.

We work in small batches, with mills we trust and tailors who've been with us since year one. No filler fabric, no rushed seams. Just clothes built to be lived in.
